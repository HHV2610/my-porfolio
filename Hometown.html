Hai Phong City

A Glimpse into the Heart of Vietnam

Location

Hải Phòng is a dynamic coastal city located in the northeastern region of Vietnam, just 120 kilometers from Hanoi, the nation's capital. Known as the “City of Flame Flowers” due to the abundance of flamboyant trees lining its streets, Hải Phòng is Vietnam's third-largest city and one of the most important ports in the country.

Hai Phong's flame flowers (Source: Collected)

People and Culture

Hải Phòng is inhabited by a population over two million people, renowned for their persistence and diligent nature. The city's culture is a distinctive combination of traditional Vietnamese values and modern influences. The residents of Hải Phòng are known for their straightforward and hardworking personality, reflecting the city’s strong heritage.

The city celebrates various traditional festivals, including the Do Son Buffalo Fighting Festival, which attracts thousands of visitors each year. This event is not just a spectacle but a enriching cultural tradition that showcases the bravery and strength admired by the locals.

Do Son Buffalo Fighting Festival (Source: Collected)

Scenery

Hai Phong's natural beauty is diverse and enchanting, providing a harmonious blend of urban charm and landscape. The city's suburbs are dotted with lush meadows, scenic rivers, and tranquil lakes that provide a peaceful escape from bustling city life. Hai Phong's coastline is truly breathtaking, with endless stretches of sandy beaches and crystal clear waters.

Do Son is one of the most famous tourist destinations in the North in general, as well as Hai Phong in particular. The Do Son tourist area is a resort that boasts numerous beaches, stunning scenery, and majestic mountains. Do Son Beach is most beautiful at dawn. When the sun has just risen behind the ships, it is a wonderful time to relax, swim, check in, and explore the lives of the fishing village people in the early morning.

The tranquil shores of Do Son Beach (Source: Collected)

If you are looking for a great destination to relax and explore, Cat Ba is definitely an option not to be missed. Located in the Cat Ba archipelago, in Hai Phong city, Cat Ba is not only famous for its wild beauty, but also for its long beaches, majestic limestone mountains and rich ecosystem. With the perfect combination of majestic nature and diverse entertainment activities, Cat Ba is truly a paradise in the middle of the sea, waiting for you to explore and enjoy. Cat Ba National Park, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, is home to many rare and endangered species, making it a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts. The island also offers various outdoor activities, including hiking, kayaking, and exploring hidden caves, making it a perfect destination for those looking to connect with nature.

Cat Ba Island (Source: Collected)

Cuisine

Hải Phòng's cuisine is a feast for the senses, with a focus on fresh seafood. The city's specialty is Bánh Đa Cua, a crab noodle soup that is rich in flavor and a true representation of the region's traditional food. Other local delicacies include Nem Cua Bể (crab spring rolls) and Lẩu Cua Đồng (crab hotpot), each dish highlighting the fresh, local ingredients from the sea.

A steaming bowl of Bánh Đa Cua, Hải Phòng's signature dish (Source: Collected)

Conclusion

Hải Phòng City is a destination where a wealth of cultural legacy meets stunning natural beauty. Whether you are visiting its ancient sites, enjoying its delicious cuisine, or simply admiring the beautiful landscapes, Hải Phòng offers an experience that is both authentic and unforgettable.
